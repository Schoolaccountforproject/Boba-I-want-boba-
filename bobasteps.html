Simple Steps to Make Boba Tea

  1. Boil the Boba Pearls: Cook the tapioca pearls according to the package instructions. It usually takes 20-30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Tea Base: Brew your favorite teaâ€”black tea, green tea, or jasmine tea are great choices.
  3. Add Sweeteners: Mix sugar or flavored syrups into your tea while it's warm to enhance the flavor.
  4. Mix and Serve: Combine the cooked boba pearls with the tea. Add ice and milk if desired.This is the best time to add your favorite toppings!
  5. Enjoy: Pour into a glass, insert a wide straw, and enjoy your homemade boba tea!
